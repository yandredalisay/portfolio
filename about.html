About  Us

Mission

Mindanao State University - Iligan Institute of Technology Official Esports Organization: The Titans Esports envisions the establishment of a gaming community in Mindanao State University - Iligan Institute of Technology that promotes inclusivity for student gamers who have a passion for Esports. To further develop individuals in teamwork and communication in its gamers and to create safe, regulated, and responsible esports entertainment with a positive impact on society.

Vision

Titans Esports strives in creating one of the best campus esports organizations, both on the local and national stage, with a healthy and inclusive community for student gamers to break the stigma and barriers that stall the emergence of student gamers. Titans Esports visions itself to expand its connections and involve itself in nation-building that promotes camaraderie and holistic development of student gamers through integrity, collaboration, and character development.
